AIMS

The aim of Reach-A-Smile Initiative is to improve oral health awareness and access to basic dental care among disadvantaged school-aged children by combating dental illiteracy and promoting healthy dental practices in underserved communities.

OBJECTIVES

  1. Educate disadvantaged school-aged children on proper dental hygiene practices.
  2. Increase awareness of the importance of oral health from an early age within disadvantaged school communities.
  3. Provide access to basic dental hygiene products to children in unreached and rural schools.
  4. Reduce dental illiteracy by delivering grassroots-level oral health education.
  5. Promote healthier, fairer, and better-informed communities through sustained oral health advocacy.
